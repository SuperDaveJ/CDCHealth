Interacting With the Course

This course is designed to make your learning experience interactive, engaging, and meaningful. You will find that this course has a variety of different interactions, including selectable elements, audio narration, and slideshows.

Selectable elements have been included in the course to provide additional content and an increased level of interactivity. For example, whenever you see highlighted text, you must select it to view more information in a popup.

You have already seen one example of a selectable graphic. Throughout the course, you will see other graphics you can select to launch audio, popup text, tables, or documents.
