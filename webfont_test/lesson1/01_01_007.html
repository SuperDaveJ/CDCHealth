Lesson 1

Getting Around the Course

Lesson Summary

This course will equip you to enhance CDC’s public health mission and capacity to do effective health policy related work by helping to foster a common understanding of what policy is and the process by which it is conceptualized and developed.

To help you retain the information you are learning so you can apply it in your job, you can practice the content presented by completing Knowledge Reviews.

Now that you have learned how to navigate through the course successfully and what the course expectations are, you are ready to get started learning more about policy analysis.

The goal of Lesson 2 is to provide you with a brief overview of health policy at CDC. We will discuss CDC’s definition of policy, the policy analysis competency, the policy process, and how policy impacts health.
