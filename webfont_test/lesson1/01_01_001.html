Lesson Overview

In this first lesson, you will explore the course features. By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Identify the course structure.
  • Identify how to use the course materials.

Once you are through with this lesson, select Lesson 2: Overview of Health Policy, on the course menu.

This lesson should take approximately 5 minutes to complete.
