Knowledge Reviews

Throughout each module, you will check your knowledge by completing Knowledge Reviews. Knowledge Reviews are questions that are designed to let you test your understanding and retention of what you've learned. You will be able to read feedback about your answers that will help you understand why particular choices are correct or incorrect.

Most Knowledge Review questions allow two attempts. If you answer incorrectly on your first attempt, you will be prompted to try again.
