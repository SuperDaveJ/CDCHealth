Lesson 3

Define the Problem Which Impacts Public Health

Lesson Overview

Lesson 2 discussed various factors related to policy analysis at CDC. You learned about CDC’s definition of policy, its policy process and how this process aligns with the CDC Health Policy Competency. For example, for policy analysis, defining the problem (fundamental to this lesson), corresponds with the first step portrayed in the policy process. You also learned about CDC’s role in policy development, and the impact of both current and future public health issues on policy development.

The objectives for this lesson include:

  • Identify the health problem.
  • Describe the key components of a problem definition.
  • Determine the health policy problem scope.
  • Identify sources of information to identify the causes of the problem.
  • Identify stakeholders and their perspectives on the problem.

This lesson should take approximately 25 minutes to complete.
