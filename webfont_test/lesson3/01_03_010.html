Lesson 3

Define the Problem Which Impacts Public Health

Stakeholders’ Perspectives on the Problem

Part 1

Stakeholder perspectives are an important part of the entire policy analysis process.

Stakeholders can be involved in crafting the problem definition by providing context to the problem that you may not have obtained through the literature. They can be important sources for supplementing your knowledge of the “who, what, when, where, and how” questions by adding local context to, or telling stories about, the issue.
