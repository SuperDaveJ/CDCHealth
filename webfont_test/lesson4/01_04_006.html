Conducting an Environmental Scan

Exploring Available Options

Although it is common to begin a search for policies to address public health problems by reviewing the public health peer-reviewed literature, additional sources for environmental scans could also include public health grey literature and policy documents in other sectors.

For example, the graphic on the right depicts examples of policy option sources from other sectors that could have relevance for public health problems.

When exploring available policy options to address a public health problem, it is important to be proactive—the earlier information is identified, the better.
