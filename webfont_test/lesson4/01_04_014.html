Lesson 4

Identifying Policy Options

Lesson Summary

In this lesson, you learned how to conduct an environmental scan for potential policy options, how to identify those options, and how to narrow down the options so that you can focus your in-depth analysis on the few with the greatest potential. At this stage of your analysis, you should have a pool of potential policy options for further analysis.

You should now be able to:

  • Explain how to conduct an environmental scan for potential options.
  • Identify a range of potential policy options.
  • Select potential policy options for further analysis.

In Lesson 5, you will learn how to select, prioritize, and apply selected criteria to the pool of policy options you are considering.
