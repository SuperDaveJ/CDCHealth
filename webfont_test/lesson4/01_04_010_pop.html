Six-question Framework

  1. What is the important trend related to the public health problem of interest? 
    What is the shape of this trend over the past several years?
  2. Who are the stakeholders concerned about the trend?
  3. Why is this trend happening? (What is the cause? What is responsible?)
  4. Where is there policy leverage to address the underlying cause of the trend?
  5. How will the policy work?  How will it play out over time?
    How might unintended consequences occur? 
    How might the policy positively or negatively impact …
    1. Health status?
    2. Health spending?
    3. Health care system?
    4. Health equity?
  6. When would the policy create an impact on health status? 
    When would you see an improvement in some other indicators (i.e., spending, services)?
