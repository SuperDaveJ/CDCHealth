Lesson 4

Identifying Policy Options

Does the Problem Have Potential Policy Solutions?

Reflecting on the different factors that affect health can help determine the interventions that can be used to address a problem. There are often policy opportunities within different categories, or types of interventions (for example, long-lasting interventions and changing the context), that may have a larger impact.

For example, there are many infection control guidelines that healthcare facilities can use to address the clinical context of healthcare-associated infections. However, the guidelines currently contain many recommendations which are further prioritized according to the evidence and effectiveness of interventions. This provides an opening for policy to be created and refined.
