Effective Communication of Selected Policy Options

Selecting Appropriate Communication Medium

An op-ed is also a communications medium by which CDC can communicate about policy priorities; however, this would only be utilized by those at the highest level in the agency.

Communication materials can be shared through various means including:

  • CDC documents such as:
    • Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report (MMWR)
    • Hill Alerts
    • Vital Signs
  • Social media
    • YouTube
    • Facebook
    • Twitter
  • CDC websites
  • Partner websites
  • Stakeholder meetings

For more information on communication guidelines and strategies, be sure to check the CDCU communications courses available to you.
