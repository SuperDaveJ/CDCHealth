Guidance

  • State the evidence of a policy’s impact.
  • In certain documents (e.g., Vital Signs), recommend what policies, states, or organizations “can implement” to have a specific health impact after specifying the impact documented in the available scientific evidence.
  • Documents may recommend that organizations consider the health impact when evaluating a certain policy and/or deciding on the adoption of a certain policy. Such a recommendation is more aligned with our mandate and expertise than prescribing specific policy action.
  • Any statements on practice or policy should be directly related to findings of the investigation or analysis.
