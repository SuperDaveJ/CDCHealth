Lesson 7

Effective Communication of Selected Policy Options

CDC Limitations in Communicating Policy Options

Framing Messages for a Wide Audience—Part 1

You should understand that many decision-makers will not just rely on the science and evidence related to selected policy options. You should pay careful attention to how you communicate the options to ensure decision-makers understand your suggestions and give them proper consideration.

The National Prevention Strategy and associated materials provide good examples of messaging, as do Communities Putting Prevention to Work (CPPW) grant success stories.
