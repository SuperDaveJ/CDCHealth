Using Federal funds to develop and/or disseminate materials that exhibit all three of the following characteristics is prohibited: (1) refer to specific legislation or other order; (2) reflect a point of view on that legislation or other order; and (3) contain an overt call to action.

There is guidance provided to assist participants in understanding this complex topic. Participants should also consult with organizational leadership, policy staff (including CDC Washington), and/or OGC for additional clarification on appropriate roles and activities for CDC.
