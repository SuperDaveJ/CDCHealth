Effective Communication of Selected Policy Options

CDC Limitations in Communicating Policy Option(s)

Communications must not violate prohibitions on lobbying—Part 1

Prohibitions on lobbying limit how CDC communicates about policy options.

To whom does the limitation apply? Limitations apply to CDC/HHS employees, grant recipients, subrecipients, and agents acting for such recipients or subrecipients, including participants of conferences supported by Federal funds and cooperative agreements.

What is the overall limitation? Federal funds may not be used in any part for any activity designed to influence the enactment of legislation or other orders proposed or pending before the Congress, any state government, state legislature, local legislature, or legislative body. Partners may have sources of other funding which do not necessarily have the same restrictions.
