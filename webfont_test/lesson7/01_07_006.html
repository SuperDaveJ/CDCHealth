Part 2

An important aspect of the CDC Forecasting Initiative and the CDC Forecasting Portal is that they help promote policy awareness and issues management within CDC programs and offices, and provide them with a useful tool to enhance their own environmental scanning, forecasting, and planning capacity and needs. Documents and other products which are intended for policy awareness, as well as those which may require review, are to be listed in the Forecasting Portal.

When preparing materials to communicate policy options with external audiences, a draft version of the communication product(s) should be submitted to the CDC Forecasting Portal. It is possible that the product may then be revised for content and/or dissemination method. The product should also be submitted for policy awareness and review at this time.
