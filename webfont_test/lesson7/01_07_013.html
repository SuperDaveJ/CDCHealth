Lesson 7

Effective Communication of Selected Policy Options

Policy Awareness and Review

Remember that documents intended for external audiences which contain policy recommendations must be reviewed prior to dissemination outside CDC. The CDC Forecasting Portal is CDC’s system for raising awareness about documents that should be brought to leadership attention, including those that may require a policy review. The goals of reviewing policy documents before dissemination outside CDC are to improve CDC’s ability to:

  • Keep CDC senior leadership and HHS abreast of policy efforts across CDC.
  • Identify opportunities to advance policies, leverage support, and mitigate risks.
  • Ensure that policy analyses and recommendations are based on sound evidence, are timely and sensitive to current conditions, and are disseminated appropriately.
  • Assess compatibility of new policy documents and recommendations with current CDC, HHS, or other federal agency policy so that areas of difference can be appropriately addressed.
  • Keep leadership abreast of new and emerging scientific evidence that might lead to eventual changes in current policies.
  • Ensure CDC adheres to the anti-lobbying restrictions placed upon it by Congress in its annual appropriations and in other legislation.
  • Minimize burden and redundancy by utilizing the CDC Forecasting Portal and other existing processes to identify and address policy efforts.
