Effective Communication of Selected Policy Options

Selecting the Appropriate Communication Method

There are many different ways to communicate selected policy options. After summarizing potential policy options, weighing the options against selected criteria (such as health impact, economic impact, and feasibility), and selecting a list of policy options to share with others, you will communicate these options to others. This communication may occur through policy briefs, memos, presentations or briefings, press releases or media advisories, podcasts, and tool kits.
