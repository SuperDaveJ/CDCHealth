Lesson 7

Effective Communication of Selected Policy Options

Lesson Summary

In previous lessons, you learned about the first two domains of the policy process: problem identification and policy analysis. You then further explored policy analysis when you learned how to define a health problem, identify policy options, assess the evidence, and involve stakeholders in the process. During this lesson, you identified methods to communicate policy options.

The objectives for this lesson included:

  • Identify various formats for communicating policy analysis and recommendations.
  • Describe CDC’s roles and limitations in communicating policy options and recommendations.
  • Familiarize yourself with CDC’s policy awareness and review process and the CDC Forecasting Portal.

Proceed to the next lesson for a summary of the topics we’ve covered in this course.
