Lesson 7

Effective Communication of Selected Policy Options

CDC Limitations in Communicating Policy Option(s)

Communications must not violate prohibitions on lobbying—Part 2

Specific elements of this limitation that have relevance for communications include prohibitions against:

  • Inducing members of the public to contact their elected representatives to urge support of, or opposition to, proposed or pending legislation or other orders.
  • Affecting the opinions of the general public or any segment of the population if the communications refer to specific legislation and/or other orders, directly express a view on such legislation or other orders, and encourage the audience to take action with respect to the matter.
  • Communications that directly express a view on proposed or pending legislation and other orders and which are directed to members, staff, or other employees of a legislative body or to government officials or employees who participate in the formulation of legislation or other orders.
