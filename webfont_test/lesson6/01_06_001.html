Lesson 6

Selecting Policy Options

Lesson Overview

After you and your team have assessed the potential policy options and prioritized your criteria, you are ready to compare the policy options, and use your professional judgment to apply criteria to them, then select one or more to share with stakeholders and decision-makers.

The objectives for this lesson include:

  • Compare and contrast policy options according to criteria.
  • Describe the role of the policy analyst.
  • Select viable policy options or a combination of options for the identified demographic(s).

This lesson should take approximately 20 minutes to complete.
