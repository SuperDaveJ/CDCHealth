Lesson 6

Selecting Policy Options

Lesson Summary

At this stage in the analysis, you have assessed the potential policy options according to your rank-order criteria and compared the policy options. You have used your professional judgment to apply criteria to them, to select one or more to share with stakeholders and decision-makers.

You should now be able to:

  • Compare and contrast policy options according to criteria.
  • Describe the role of the policy analyst.
  • Select viable policy options or a combination of options for the identified demographic(s).

Now that you’ve selected policy options, the next step, covered in the upcoming lesson, is to identify methods to effectively communicate your recommendations to decision-makers.
