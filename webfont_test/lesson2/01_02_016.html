Lesson 2

Overview of Health Policy

Lesson Summary

The focus of this lesson was to discuss various factors related to policy analysis at CDC. You should now be able to:

  • Describe CDC’s definition of policy.
  • Identify the elements of the policy analysis competency.
  • Identify the elements of the policy process.
  • Describe how policy impacts health.

The next lesson will discuss in more detail the first step of the policy process – problem identification. You will learn how to use reliable and accurate information sources to identify and assess a public health problem or issue.

For the purposes of this course, problem and issue are interchangeable.
