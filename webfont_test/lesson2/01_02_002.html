What is Policy?

One potentially effective way to improve the health of populations is through policy. Policy development is one of three core public health functions (Institute of Medicine, 1988) and Ten Essential Public Health Services (Core Public Health Functions Steering Committee, 1994).

At CDC, we define policy as a law, regulation, procedure, administrative action, incentive, or voluntary practice of governments and other institutions.
