In Lesson 1, you learned how to navigate through the course successfully, and what to expect to learn in the subsequent lessons. The focus of Lesson 2 is to discuss various factors related to policy analysis at CDC. Gaining a clearer understanding of CDC’s role in policy development, policy as a whole, CDC’s specific policy process, and the impact of both current and future public health issues on policy development is one of the first steps you can take to play your part in helping to enhance CDC’s public health mission and capacity to do effective health policy work.
