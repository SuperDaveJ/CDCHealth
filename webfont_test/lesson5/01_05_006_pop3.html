Fiscal and Economic Impact

  • What are costs associated with the policy?
    • For public (federal, state, local) and private systems to enact, implement, and enforce the policy?
    • For individuals?
  • What is the value of the benefits (i.e., economic value of the health outcomes)?
  • How do the costs compare to the benefits?
    • What are the cost savings or return on investment (ROI)?
    • How are costs and benefits distributed?
    • What is the timeline for budgetary and economic return on investment?
  • What is the funding source?
  • Are additional taxes or appropriations required?
