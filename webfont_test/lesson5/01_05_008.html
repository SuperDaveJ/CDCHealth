Lesson 5

Assessing Policy Options

Lesson Summary

In this lesson, we delved a little further into the second domain of the policy process and provided you with information on how to rank your policy options under consideration.

During this lesson, you learned to:

  • Establish criteria for evaluating policy options based on desired outcomes.
  • Assign weights to each criterion to reflect priorities.
  • Apply criteria to selected potential policy options.

In the next lesson, we will discuss how to prioritize policy options so you can select one or more for your recommendations.
