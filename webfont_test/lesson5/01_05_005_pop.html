Detail Information

The State of Avalon had 100 deaths from motorcycle crashes in the previous year. More than 10 peer-reviewed published studies have consistently shown that helmet laws reduce motorcycle fatalities and emergency room visits. One economic analysis revealed cost savings for the state from helmet use ranges from $50-1600 per registered motorcycle. The medical and public health establishment supports mandatory helmet legislation in Avalon; however, the political opposition to government mandates is strong. Currently, there is no champion for the helmet legislation, but there is a very organized opposition.
