Lesson 5

Assessing Policy Options

Lesson Overview

In Lesson 4, you learned how to conduct an environmental scan for potential policy options and how to use that information to select policy options for further analysis.

This lesson delves a little further into the second domain of the policy process and provides you with information on how to identify and apply appropriate analysis tools and techniques to analyze policy options under consideration.

During this lesson, you will learn to:

  • Establish criteria for evaluating policy options based on desired outcomes.
  • Assign weights to each criterion to reflect priorities.
  • Apply criteria to selected potential policy options.

This lesson should take approximately 10 minutes to complete.
