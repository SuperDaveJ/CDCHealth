Public Health Impact

  • How does the policy address the problem or issue (e.g., increase access, protect from exposure)?
  • What are the magnitude, reach, impact, and distribution of benefit and burden (including impact on risk factor, quality of life, morbidity, and mortality)?
    • What population(s) will benefit? How? When?
    • What population(s) will be negatively impacted? How? When?
  • Will the policy impact health disparities / health equity? How?
  • What are the projected short- and long-term outcomes?
  • Are there gaps in the data/evidence-base?
