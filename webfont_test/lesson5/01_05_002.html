Lesson 5

Assessing Policy Options

Establishing Criteria for Evaluating Policy Options

At this point in your analysis, you have compiled a manageable number of potential policy options to look at. These options have been significantly narrowed down through the environmental scan you conducted, in order to properly align with your specific problem definition and scope, as well as windows of opportunity.

Next, they must be assessed against criteria you establish (to help determine initial viability). Then you'll compare them against each other to determine the strengths and weaknesses of each, and to build an analysis that clearly illustrates the trade-offs in each option.
